Secure Copilot

About Us

Secure Copilot is an AI-powered assistant designed to enhance driver safety and engagement during journeys. It monitors the driver for drowsiness and fatigue, alerting them with a personalized voice from a loved one. To keep the driver awake and alert, the assistant engages in discussions about news and various topics and supports casual conversations. It further supports multiple languages, and ensures adherence to various safety protocols like seat belt use, phone avoidance etc. In emergencies, it can alert nearby police and rescue teams, ensuring immediate assistance.
